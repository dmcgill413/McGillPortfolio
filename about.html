Who I am

I’m a developer based in Fishers, Indiana, currently studying web development and database design. I enjoy building clean layouts, solving technical problems, and learning new tools that help me create better digital experiences. I also have a passion for digital art and design, which allows me to bring a creative perspective to my development work. In my free time, I like to explore new technologies, contribute to open-source projects, and create digital illustrations inspired by my favorite artists. Before stepping into the world of web development, I am currently a Microbiology Supervisor for Indiana University Health, where I manage a team of laboratory professionals and oversee the daily operations of the microbiology department. This role has honed my leadership skills, attention to detail, and ability to work under pressure, all of which are invaluable in my development projects.
